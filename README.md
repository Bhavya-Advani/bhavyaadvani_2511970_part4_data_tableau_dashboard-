
**Calculated Field**	**Required Logic**
**Profit Margin:**	Profit divided by sales
**Cost:**	Sales minus profit
**Average Sales Value:**	Sales divided by quantity per customer id
**Customer rating bucket:** High customer rating if rating is greater than 4.5; Moderate customer rating if rating between 3 to 3.5; and low customer rating if rating below 3
**Delivery Bucket**: delivery same day if delivery days are 0; delivery within a week if delivery days are between 1 to 7; delivery beyond week if delivery days are greater than 7
